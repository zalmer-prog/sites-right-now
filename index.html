Questions people ask.

What does the $75 actually cover?

The design and build, the hosting, SSL, and free small updates after launch — all in for one flat price. The only extra is the domain name's yearly registration fee (usually $10–20), which is paid to the registrar rather than to me. I'll help you pick one and get it set up. If anything else ever costs extra, I'll tell you the number before I do any work.

How long until I'm online?

Usually about a week from the time I have your details. Simpler sites can be quicker.

Do I own my domain?

Yes. It's registered for your business and it's yours. If you ever decide to leave, you take it with you.

What if I need more than one page?

That's fine. Extra pages, online booking, a shop, a blog — tell me what you're after and I'll quote a fair add-on. No pressure to take it.

Is there a contract?

No contract and no setup fee. It's month to month, and you can cancel any time.

I already have a domain. Can you use it?

Of course. I'll connect the one you've got — and knock a little off the price since I'm not registering a new one.
